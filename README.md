# Credit Card Financial Dashboard (Power BI)

## 📊 Project Objective
To develop a **comprehensive credit card weekly dashboard** that provides **real-time insights** into key performance metrics and trends, enabling stakeholders to **monitor and analyze credit card operations effectively**.

---

## 🛠 Tech Stack
- **Power BI** – Data modeling, DAX, visualization  
- **SQL** – Data extraction & transformation  
- **Excel/CSV** – Data preparation & staging  
- **PDF Reports** – Static distribution of insights  

---

## 📂 Project Files
| File | Description |
|------|-------------|
| `Credit_Card_Report-Customer.pdf` | Customer-centric KPIs such as revenue by income, job, education, age, marital status, state, and dependents |
| `Credit_Card_Report-Transaction.pdf` | Transaction-centric KPIs such as revenue, transaction amount, volume, card category, and expense type |

---

## 🔑 Key Metrics
- **Revenue** – ₹55.3M total (across all card categories)
- **Transaction Volume** – ~655.7K transactions
- **Transaction Amount** – ₹44.5M
- **Interest Earned** – ₹7.8M
- **Customer Income** – ₹576M (aggregate across all profiles)

---

## 📈 Insights & Findings

### Customer Segment Insights (from `Customer` report)
- **Top Earning Occupation:** Businessmen generate the **highest revenue (~₹17.4M)** followed by Govt & Self-employed.
- **Age Group Performance:** **60+** segment leads with ~₹14M revenue, followed by 30-40 (~₹11M).
- **Education Impact:** Post-graduates and graduates together contribute **~₹22M+** in revenue.
- **State-wise Revenue:** **TX, NJ, CA, NY, FL** form the top 5 revenue states.
- **Dependents:** Customers with **2–4 dependents** show stronger revenue (~₹9M).

### Transaction Behavior Insights (from `Transaction` report)
- **Card Category:** Blue cards dominate with **₹46M (~83%)** of total revenue; Gold & Platinum are niche segments.
- **Payment Mode:** **Swipe (₹35M)** is still the most common, but **Chip (₹17M)** adoption is strong.
- **Expense Type:** Bills (~₹14M) and Entertainment (~₹10M) are top spending categories.
- **Quarterly Trend:** Revenue and transaction volume are steady across Q1–Q4, signaling consistent card usage.

---

## 🧩 Dashboard Features
- Drill-downs by **Week, Quarter, Card Category, Job, Age, State**
- Interactive slicers for **Education, Marital Status, Income Level**
- KPIs showing **Revenue, Interest Earned, Transactions**
- Clear **customer vs transaction** segmentation for decision-making
